Foundry

Alternatype is an indie typefoundry founded in 2015 by Letterer and self-taught type designer Akufadhl.
Based in Indonesia. He currently studying in Faculty of Arabic Language at Al-Azhar University Cairo, He is interested in Lettering and Calligraphy since a young age. Crafting type with passion and joy.
He is amazed by an old and good type, good food, nothing is better than drawing type and having a good food.
